Newbie Guide

2024-10-30



Newbie Guide 101

1. After creating a user account,

2. Create a character.



3. Open all the items in your inventory.

4. In the Weapon [Selection] Box, choose the weapon suitable for your character's class. For example, if your character class is Extreme, select the [Bow].

5. Equip all the Free Donate items in your inventory. Once equipped, you're ready to level up.

6. To access the Leveling Spot Map, locate the NPC "College Representative":

Click on "Bus Station", then select "Death Prison 3F".

Set the station to "Entrance", and hit "OK" to teleport to the map.



7. Join or create a party for faster leveling:

If you can't find a party to join, create one by using the chat command: /recruit<space>.

Assign your skills to the [F4] slot, then press [F5] to activate Auto Skill.

8. Once you reach Level 200+, you can start rebirthing using the "1-70 Reborn Card" in your inventory.

Click on "Reborn", then press Tab to refresh your EXP.

Repeat this process until you reach 70 Rebirths.



9. Don't forget to claim your Attendance rewards:

Once you've been online for over an hour, go to the MarketPlace (30,20) and find the NPC "Instructor".

Click on Attendance, then select today's date (e.g., for November 22, click "22").

Press "H", then go to Attendance to view the items and days required to claim rewards like Rosa, Brace, Free Gift Box, and EP Lotto Box.



10. Once you reach 70 Rebirths and Level 248+, it's best to level up in the "Fairy Tale" Map.

Click on "Bus Station", then select "Fairy Tale".

Set the station to "Entrance", and hit "OK" to teleport to the map.

11. Claim your Capreward Chestbox:

Open your bank by pressing "B", then claim the Capreward Chestbox.

Before opening it, ensure you're maximizing your leveling time to make the most of the 1-Day Rosa/Brace. Once ready, use the 8 Rosa* and 8 Brace*.

Add stats to VIT to survive better:

Use the chat command /vit 5000 to allocate stats to VIT.

Don’t worry about using stats in VIT early on since there’s a free Reset Stats Card in the Capreward Chest.



 

12. Buy cheap HP Potions from an NPC and enable the built-in Auto Pots feature in-game.



13. Upgrade your items immediately:

Focus on God of War's Helmet, Lover Heart, and Super Strength Earring using HP Cards [Max: 1500] to boost your HP significantly.

14. Reaching Level 350 and Max Reborn

Once you hit Level 350 and max reborn, you can open your Cap Reward and start hunting for your WEAPON and SET. Here are the details:

Maximum Random Values (RV) when hunting mobs:

• Weapon attack rate: 1% ~ 90.0%

• Armor defense rate: 1% ~ 30.0%

Map Locations for Hunting:

MutantB1: Armor and Weapon

MutantB2: Armor and Weapon

MutantB3: Armor and Weapon

FreePK Room2: Armor and Weapon

FairyTale: Armor and Weapon

Mechanical: Armor and Weapon

15. Setting Goals for Equipment Upgrade

Focus on prioritizing upgrades in the following order:

  1. Maximize [+Attack] on your chosen weapon.
  2. Enhance [Attack Rate %].
  3. Add [HP Card] to all your armor pieces.

16. Utilizing Freebie Weapons

While your weapon isn’t maxed, make the most of the Freebie Weapon +100 in the Dungeon.

Dungeon Requirements: • Reborn: 70
 • Level: 247~350
 • Item: Dungeon Entrance Card (available from the Dungeon Instructor)

17. Dungeon Strategy

• Step 1: Create a party and enter the dungeon. Locate and defeat the boss, Hulk, to receive a Dungeon Reward Box.
• Step 2: Select 15,000,000 Gold as the reward and sell it to any NPC or the Dungeon Entrance NPC.
Note: The Dungeon Reward Box expires quickly, so open it immediately.

Repeat Process:
• Disband your party and create a new one to re-enter the dungeon.

18. Efficient Boss Kill Tips

• Purchase Madness Box from the All-In-One Shop in Marketplace (coordinates [33,32]).
• Alternatively, use an alternate account as a tank to avoid being debuffed by the boss.

19. Fast Gold Farming

Farm gold effectively in Godroom 1F~6F. Purchase tickets from the All-In-One Shop in the Marketplace ([33,32]) using Contribution Points.

How to earn Contribution Points fast:
• Set up a vending store in the Marketplace.
• If you're max reborn, you'll earn double Contribution Points from vending (works only in the Marketplace).

20. Investing Gold in Character Upgrades

When you have enough gold, visit the All-In-One Shop (Marketplace [33,32]) and purchase upgrade cards based on your priority list in Step 15.

21. Participate in Red vs. Blue Competitions

• Schedule: 6:00 PM ~ 6:30 PM and 10:00 PM ~ 10:30 PM
• Rewards: Win or lose, you'll receive materials for crafting Epirus Gear, essential for character upgrades.

22. Join a Guild

• Participate in Club Wars to improve your combat skills and learn strategies from your guildmates.

23. Practice Combat

• For advanced skill mastery, practice with friends or teammates in the Practicing Yard.
